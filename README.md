# Privacy Policy – Court Radar

Last updated: 2026

Court Radar helps users discover nearby basketball courts using map services and publicly available geographic data.

## Information We Collect

The app may access the following information:

- **Location Data** – Used to identify basketball courts near your current location.
- **Internet Access** – Required to retrieve court data from public map services.

Court Radar does **not collect, store, or sell personal information**.

## How We Use Information

Location data is used only to:

- Show nearby basketball courts
- Enable navigation to courts
- Improve map functionality

Location data is **not stored on our servers**.

## Third-Party Services

The app uses the following services:

- Google Maps
- OpenStreetMap

These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

## Data Sharing

Court Radar **does not sell or share personal data** with third parties.

## Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data, but no system is completely secure.

## Children's Privacy

Court Radar does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be posted on this page.
